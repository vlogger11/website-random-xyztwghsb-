About Us

Earth One is a flagship development by Earth Developers.

A strategic partnership between United Builders and Lalit Roongta Group.

The Partnership

Setting New Standards in Gated Communities

With a combined legacy spanning over ten decades across various segments of the real estate industry, United Builders and Lalit Roongta Group set out to establish new standards in developing land into gated communities where every contour, every vista and every shared space reflects thoughtful design and lasting purpose.

Both organizations bring decades of legacy, trust, and goodwill in the Nashik real estate landscape, with a proven track record of delivering landmark projects that stand the test of time.

Pioneering Growth

United Builders: A Legacy of Trust

Established in 1968, United Builders has been pioneering the growth of Nashik with over 100 successfully delivered projects across various asset classes.

Founded by Late Mr. Asan Daryani, the group is now run by second and third generation entrepreneurs.

At United Builders, we believe real estate is a legacy of trust. Rooted in family values and guided by the principle that the consumer is king, we create thoughtfully planned, high-quality spaces that balance functionality, aesthetics, and integrity—built to endure across generations.

Our aim is to consistently raise the benchmark of real estate development in Nashik by delivering timeless residential, commercial, and plotted developments. Our ambition is to build long-lasting relationships, shape enduring landmarks, and carry forward a multi-generational legacy driven by excellence, transparency, and purposeful growth.

Uncompromising Quality

Lalit Roongta Group: Translating Aspirations

Since 1996, the Lalit Roongta Group has been translating aspirations of owning real estate into enduring landmarks, defined by uncompromising quality, thoughtful design, and timely execution.

A family-owned enterprise guided by trust and transparency, the Group creates environments that seamlessly blend luxury with purpose.

By integrating enduring values with advanced construction technologies, the Lalit Roongta Group has emerged as one of Nashik’s most respected real estate names. Creating spaces that elevate lifestyles and stand as legacies for generations.

Our Vision

At Earth One

At Earth One, our vision is to transform the very idea of what a gated community should be – designed to inspire belonging, ownership and legacy.

With a combined legacy spanning ten decades across various segments of the real estate industry, United Builders and Lalit Roongta Group set out to set new standards in developing land into gated communities where every contour, every vista and every shared space reflects thoughtful design and lasting purpose. Earth One is our expression of that vision - a gated community that embodies the spirit of what community living can and should be - meaningful and beautiful.

We found the perfect setting to bring this vision to life in Igatpuri, one of India’s most promising retreats, where rolling hills, green farmlands, tranquil lakes and clear skies meet the momentum of growth. A destination that is still peaceful and now rising to be a perfect escape with new connectivity. We chose the perfect location with intent, located between the Samruddhi Expressway and the Ghoti-Sinnar National Highway, to give residents a rare balance of effortless connectivity and natural beauty, where the views hold your dreams. We are delivering a world-class campus where community living is timeless. At the heart of the project stands the Earth One Club - a living expression of Earth One’s spirit, where sport, wellness, socialising and culture come alive in perfect harmony.

Every element we create is guided by a single purpose: to shape a living environment that lasts beyond a lifetime - a legacy of beauty, belonging, and quiet confidence, delivering value for generations to come.
